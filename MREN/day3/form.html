Naming convention

camelCase
multiple words are joined by making the first letter capital (except first word)
ex. hello manoj ==> helloManoj
kebab-case / hyphen-case
multiple words are joined by '-' in between
ex. hello manoj ==> hello-manoj
snake_case
multiple words are joined by '_' in between
ex. hello manoj ==> hello_manoj
